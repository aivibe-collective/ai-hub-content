Model Selection Guide
  • Flash models (e.g., gemini-1.5-flash): Faster and more efficient, good for simple content generation tasks.
  • Pro models (e.g., gemini-1.5-pro): More powerful, better for complex reasoning and detailed content.
  • Experimental models (e.g., gemini-2.5-pro-exp-03-25): Latest capabilities but may have limitations or be less stable.
Note on Preview Models

Models with "preview" in their name (e.g., gemini-2.5-pro-preview-03-25) don't have a free quota tier. Use experimental versions (e.g., gemini-2.5-pro-exp-03-25) for free tier access.
